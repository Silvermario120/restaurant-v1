About Mickey D's

McDonald’s franchise was founded by Ray Kroc in 1955. McDonald’s is a 24 hour Fast Food Restaurant. They Offer Breakfast and Lunch throughout the day. This fast food restaurant makes it a place for adults and kids with their mascot Ronald McDonald. The McDonald’s franchise are consistent when adding new things to the menu for their happy customers to try. They have a variety of selections on their menu and offer low prices with their 1$,2$,and 3$ menu.
